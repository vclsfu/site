About Us

Vietnamese Canadian League (VCL) is founded by Vietnamese-Canadian university students at Simon Fraser University (SFU) to empower fellow peers to promote unity and bridge the gap between Vietnamese and Vietnamese Canadian traditions through leadership and community. We are dedicated to educating the whole person and empowering student leaders for the future in a just and humane world.

Our Mission

We are a cultural, nonprofit organization dedicated to the general development of the student body. Like other chapters worldwide, its purpose is to provide an environment for students with Vietnamese heritage and/or interest in Vietnam to maintain and promote Vietnamese culture and tradition, have fun, assist each other, make life-long friends, and build leadership skills all through their university careers.

Our Approach

The VCL, alongside our goals, are driven by the same principles that can be summed up by our three pillars below. Though we are an organization that focuses on the Vietnamese Canadian experience, we are honored to be able to count many members of non-Vietnamese heritage as part of our family. We welcome any and all students to come see what VCL has to offer!

Our Pillars

  • Culture:   We recognize that we have a unique Vietnamese heritage and tradition and we aim to preserve and to learn more about our culture.

  • Philanthropy:   We recognize the diverse contemporary issues facing our community and we attempt to understand them, to promote awareness about them, and to address them.
