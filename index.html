About Us

Vietnamese Canadian League (VCL) is founded by Vietnamese-Canadian university students to empower fellow peers to promote unity and bridge the gap between Vietnamese and Vietnamese Canadian traditions through leadership and community.

Our Pillars

  • Culture:   We recognize that we have a unique Vietnamese heritage and tradition and we aim to preserve and to learn more about our culture.
  • Philanthropy:   We recognize the diverse contemporary issues facing our community and we attempt to understand them, to promote awareness about them, and to address them.
  • Leadership:   We recognize and embrace the opportunities that we have been given and endeavour in empowering tomorrow’s leaders through strategic initiatives and programs.
